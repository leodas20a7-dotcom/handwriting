Verandah Reverie is a flowing copperplate calligraphy script, set at a steep 27° slant.

The letterforms carry the unbroken cadence of a steel-nib hand — connected lowercase, fine entries, slightly extravagant capitals. The strokes vary in weight as a pen-on-paper hand would, with hairline upstrokes and confident shaded downstrokes. Set at large sizes it has the composed elegance of a hand-written invitation; set tighter it reads as careful correspondence rather than ornament.

The script feels at home on a perfume label, a notary's letterhead, the inside cover of a book given as a saint's-day gift, or the printed program of a small chamber recital — anywhere the writing wants to suggest attention paid and time taken.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability.
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers.
